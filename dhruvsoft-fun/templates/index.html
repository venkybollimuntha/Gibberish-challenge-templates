Guess the Gibberish Challenge

What is the Guess the Gibberish Challenge??

Guess The Gibberish is a social media filter game on Instagram and TikTok wherein a user is presented with a series of nonsensical words

which when said aloud will sound like a well-known phrase (i.e. "ditch chews haze hum thin" translates to "did you say something").

Rules to play

    In ‘Gibberish Challenge’ a user basically needs to guess a word or phrase.
    The question shows a phrase that makes no sense at all and the user will need to read it constantly to guess the right phrase before the timer goes off.
    After the timer is over the correct answer to the Gibberish will be shown to help you find if you were right or failed miserably

Tip: The best way to guess the Gibberish is to read it aloud again and again
